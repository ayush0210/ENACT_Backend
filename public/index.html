Enhancing Talk Across Contexts (ENACT)

ENACT is an innovative mobile application designed to support and enhance parent-child communication across various contexts. The app provides real-time strategies to help parents engage in rich language interactions with their children during daily activities.

Download App Learn More

About ENACT

Enhancing Talk Across Contexts (ENACT) is a project developed by the University of Florida's Anita Zucker Center for Excellence in Early Childhood Studies. ENACT focuses on supporting parents in promoting language development with their young children during everyday activities.

The ENACT mobile application uses innovative technology to provide real-time, context-specific communication strategies to parents. When a parent enters a specific location with their child, such as a grocery store, park, or library, the app delivers customized tips and prompts designed to enhance parent-child interactions in that specific environment.

By providing support directly in the contexts where parents and children spend time together, ENACT aims to increase the quantity and quality of parent-child communications, ultimately supporting children's language development and learning.

Key Features

Location-Specific Tips

Receive customized communication strategies based on your current location, whether you're at the grocery store, park, library, or other community settings.

Child-Specific Content

Set up profiles for your children with their names, ages, and interests to receive personalized communication strategies that match their developmental stages.

Feedback System

Rate tips with thumbs up/down to help the app learn which strategies work best for you and your child, ensuring more relevant suggestions in the future.

Gamification

Earn badges and track your progress as you consistently use the app to enhance communication with your child across different contexts.

AI-Enhanced Tips

Receive AI-generated tips that incorporate your child's interests and generalize strategies across different contexts to maximize learning opportunities.

Progress Tracking

Monitor your engagement with the app and see how your interactions with your child are increasing over time across different locations.

How ENACT Works

Set Up Your Profile

Create profiles for yourself and your children, including their names, ages, and interests to receive personalized tips.

Select Your Locations

Choose the community places you frequently visit with your child, such as grocery stores, parks, libraries, or restaurants.

Receive Real-Time Tips

When you arrive at your selected locations, the app delivers context-specific communication strategies that match your child's developmental needs.

Engage With Your Child

Use the suggested strategies to enhance interactions with your child, promoting language development in everyday activities.

Provide Feedback

Rate tips using thumbs up/down to help the app learn which strategies work best for you and your child.

Track Your Progress

Earn badges for consistent usage and monitor how your interactions with your child increase over time.
